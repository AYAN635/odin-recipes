Brownie Frosting

This chocolate frosting for brownies comes from my mom. You can use this recipe on made-from-scratch brownies or spread it over boxed brownies for a special touch. It's very rich and very good!

Brownie Frosting Ingredients:

  • Butter: Unsalted butter is best for this recipe.
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder: This is the main ingredient for the frosting.
  • Milk: Whole milk is best for this recipe.
  • Vanilla extract: Pure vanilla extract is best for this recipe.
  • Confectioners' sugar: This is the main sweetener for the frosting.

Steps:

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat; stir in cocoa powder. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.
  2. Stir in milk, a little at a time, until smooth. Mix in vanilla.
  3. Gradually stir in confectioners' sugar until frosting is smooth and spreadable.
  4. Spread over cooled brownies, and let set before cutting into squares.
